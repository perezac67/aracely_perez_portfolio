3D Models & Textures

This page showcases a small collection of 3D models and texture work. From stylized creatures to character props, these assets demonstrate my early exploration into modeling, UV unwrapping, and painting surface details to bring objects to life in 3D space.

×

This is a 3D character model I created entirely on my own for a mini-game called Voloraix. I handled everything from modeling and UV mapping to texturing. My focus was on adding as much detail as possible to emphasize the character’s muscular build—carefully sculpting the anatomy and highlighting features like defined muscles and prominent veins on his head. It was a great exercise in character design and pushing texture work to enhance form and personality.

×

This is a cat character texture I designed for the game Treat Racer. While I didn’t model the cat itself, all of the textures and the overall design were created by me. The character is based on a real-life calico cat, and I made sure to capture her unique mix of colors and playful personality. The result is a vibrant, goofy racer that fits perfectly into the fun, over-the-top world of the game.

×

This is another character design I created for Treat Racer, inspired by a real-life pug I know. I brought the dog’s famously goofy features into the design—like its big, expressive eyes, squished snout, and slightly clumsy charm—to give the character a fun personality.

The goal was to stay true to the pug’s lovable quirks while making it feel right at home in the game’s chaotic racing world.
