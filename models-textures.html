3D Models & Textures

This page showcases a small collection of 3D models and texture work. From stylized creatures to character props, these assets demonstrate my early exploration into modeling, UV unwrapping, and painting surface details to bring objects to life in 3D space.

×

This is a 3D character model I created entirely on my own for a mini-game called Voloraix. I handled everything from modeling and UV mapping to texturing. My focus was on adding as much detail as possible to emphasize the character’s muscular build—carefully sculpting the anatomy and highlighting features like defined muscles and prominent veins on his head. It was a great exercise in character design and pushing texture work to enhance form and personality.
