Environment Art

This page features a collection of pixel art environments and background assets.

From natural landscapes and architectural elements to decorative props, these pieces explore different moods, settings, and styles that could be used across various game worlds.

This was a side project of mine—I had never done any building before this, but after completing it, I can confidently say that I really enjoy the process. The piece includes a cluster of futuristic buildings, each designed with small details like window patterns, signs, and rooftop structures to give them a sense of life and purpose. I paid special attention to the lighting, using a mix of neon signs and glowing accents to create a vibrant nighttime scene.

I wanted to stick with a cyberpunk aesthetic, so I leaned heavily into a color palette of purples, dark blues, violets, and magentas. These tones helped capture that signature “cool at night” atmosphere, giving the scene an edgy, futuristic vibe. Overall, it was a great learning experience, and I'm excited to keep exploring this style in future builds.

This was another personal project where I wanted to experiment with capturing a sunset scene. I focused on playing with warm, vibrant colors—mainly oranges, purples, and yellows—to create a glowing transition between day and night. The visual of light across the sky and its reflection on the water was especially important to me.

One of the biggest challenges was rendering the water. Unlike buildings or static structures, water is constantly moving, and I was used to drawing clean, solid lines. This forced me to break out of my comfort zone and think more loosely, using softer strokes and gradients to suggest ripples. In the end, it helped me see light and form in a new way. This piece was not only fun to create but also taught me a lot about mood, movement, and how to evoke a natural sense of depth using flow.

I focused on capturing the natural glow and reflections to make the scene feel alive and enchanted, like a hidden sanctuary waiting to be discovered.
