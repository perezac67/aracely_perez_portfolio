Props

This page features a variety of pixel art props and interactive game items. These objects support gameplay and world-building — from signage and tools to decorative elements and collectibles.

×

This is a backpack UI prop designed for A Warrior’s Recipe. It serves as the player's storage system, where they can keep collected items and ingredients. The design features a sturdy brown backpack with gold accents. I aimed to make it both visually appealing and easily readable within the UI, while still feeling like a natural extension of the game’s world.
